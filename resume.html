Education

Degree in Fine and Applied Arts

2004 - 2008

Nnamdi Azikiwe University Awka, Anambra State

John-Otaka Nnanna holds a degree in Fine and Applied Arts from Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka, Anambra State which he acquired between 2004 to 2008. Afterwards he proceeded to Taraba State for his one-year National Youth Service Corps (NYSC).

Art Life

Nnamdi Azikiwe University Awka, Anambra State

John has a grave passion for Artworks. He discovered this drive for drawing and painting from a very tender age. As a young boy, he drew and painted lots of pictures. Notably, he drew and painted the old fifty kobo note which appeared so real to many people. This unusual drive and talent spurred him into choosing to study Fine and Applied Arts in the higher institution where he majored in Graphics Design. Before joining the ministry, John-Otaka had Art as a full-time business. He was fully into the business of drawing, painting and graphics. Upon becoming a minister of the gospel, he continued in the pursuit of his talent while doing the kingdom business. He also uses his gift to support ministers and ministries by drawing and designing for them. He has tons of artworks to his credit both for individuals, organizations, and ministries.

Ministry

Pastor

2011 - Present

Living Faith Church

John-Otaka became a full-time pastor in the Living Faith Church on 4 November 2011. Prior to this, he has been actively involved in ministerial works with different ministries. As a pastor in the Living Faith Church, he has served in different places within and outside Nigeria. He is very passionate about soul winning. His watchwords are 'Evangelism' and 'Holiness'. John-Otaka is an ardent bible student and has impacted many lives through his teaching ministry.

Christian Life

John Otaka gave his life to Christ as a young teenager during his secondary school days. Following this, he joined the Scripture Union Christian Fellowship to help build his faith and nurture his Christian Life. His core values are dedication, truthfulness, commitment, and the fear of God. Do you need a motivator? John-Otaka is that one to run to; a purpose-driven man whose passion is to see people maximize their potentials, and become productive, thereby discovering their purpose. He is a born leader who is highly graced with exceptional leadership qualities. He is endowed with great organizational and managerial skills which makes him an outstanding leader. Hence, he achieves insurmountable feats and makes a huge impact in his endeavors. Due to his zeal for God and his leadership charisma, he was appointed the Brothers’ Coordinator of the Nigerian Christian Corpers Fellowship (NCCF) and the Corper Liaison Officer (CLO) of the Ussa L.G.A. during his service year in Taraba State. Additionally, he has handled various leadership positions and currently presides over a good number of stations in the Living Faith Church.
